# Launch Vertical Slice

> **Status: DEFERRED ROADMAP.** This scope does not authorize new production work before Operation
> Pee Break passes its second two-player couch-playtest gate. Follow `NEXT-PRODUCTION-SLICE.md`.

Goal: define what is good enough for a public-quality demo.

## Required Missions

Backyard Pack demo should include:
- Backyard Rescue
- Snack Heist
- Sock Panic
- Great Backyard Squirrel Conspiracy
- Eagle Shadow Panic
- Coyotes at the Fence

## Required Quality

Each mission must have:
- clear mission select card;
- readable objective;
- clear score feedback;
- funny clear/fail state;
- replay and next mission flow;
- controller support;
- PlayMode test coverage.

## Required Art

Minimum final-quality assets:
- Cheddar playable character;
- Cocoa playable character;
- squirrel;
- coyote;
- eagle shadow;
- backyard props;
- mission UI;
- score/VFX UI.

## Required Audio

Minimum:
- bark set;
- pickup cue;
- warning cue;
- success cue;
- fail cue;
- mission select cue;
- light loopable mission music.

## Required UX

- first launch starts at mission select;
- no debug explanation needed;
- restart is immediate;
- session summary is understandable;
- no broken placeholder text.

## Not Required

- full campaign;
- all house missions;
- online features;
- cloud saves;
- final monetization;
- Apple TV build.

## Demo Definition

A launch-quality vertical slice is ready when two new players can play all Backyard Pack missions for 20 minutes and understand the objectives, controls, replay flow, and comedy without developer explanation.
