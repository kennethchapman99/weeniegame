# Difficulty Framework

> **Status: DEFERRED.** Difficulty expansion waits for human couch-playtest evidence and the
> Operation Pee Break acceptance gate.

Goal: increase mastery without increasing frustration.

## Difficulty Axes

Every mission should scale using the same levers.

### Time Pressure

Examples:
- shorter timer
- faster hazard cycle
- faster squirrel routes

### Decision Pressure

Examples:
- more route choices
- more fake-outs
- additional objectives

### Coordination Pressure

Examples:
- both dogs required
- tighter timing windows
- more role switching

### Execution Pressure

Examples:
- narrower bark windows
- more precise movement
- less recovery time

## Star Targets

1 Star
- finish mission

2 Stars
- finish with good score

3 Stars
- finish with excellent score and challenge objective

## Rule

Do not increase difficulty primarily through health, damage, or punishment.

Increase difficulty through:
- information
- timing
- teamwork
- decision making

## Mission Examples

Squirrel Conspiracy:
- more fake-outs
- faster route changes

Eagle Shadow Panic:
- less cover time
- more rescue pressure

Coyotes at the Fence:
- more simultaneous weak spots
- stronger isolation penalties
