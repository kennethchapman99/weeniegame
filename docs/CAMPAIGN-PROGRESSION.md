## Stars

Every mission awards:
- 1 star = clear
- 2 stars = strong score
- 3 stars = excellent score

Stars unlock new missions.

No paywall.
No grind currency.

## Rank System

Existing ranks become long-term bragging rights.

Examples:
- Certified Weenie
- Backyard Hero
- Squirrel Investigator
- Fence Guardian
- Pool Professional
- Snack Scientist
- Couch Monarch

## Replay Goals

Each mission should include:
- clear objective;
- score objective;
- funny challenge objective.

Example:
Squirrel Conspiracy
- find stash;
- score 1500;
- zero fake-outs.

## Completion

Campaign completion is based on stars earned, not mission count alone.

Target:
- casual players can finish;
- completionists chase perfect stars and ranks.
